Department Vision


To produce global standard ethical professionals, innovators, and entrepreneurs having strong knowledge and urge to learn latest technologies in the field of computer engineering.

Department Mission


The department continuously strives
-- To pursue excellence in the area of Computer Engineering and develop students with strong foundations, able to adapt with rapidly changing technologies through effective Teaching- Learning Process.
-- To develop competent professionals for global market with the spirit of self-study, team work, innovation and ethics among them.
-- To promote continuous learning, entrepreneurial skills and research abilities amongst the students.
